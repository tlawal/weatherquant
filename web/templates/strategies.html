URGENT EXIT — Debounced + Spread-Guarded + Confidence-Gated
Every 5 min, the signal engine computes model_prob for each bucket from NWS, WU, HRRR, and GFS forecasts. The bucket with the highest probability is "consensus." If consensus shifts away from your held bucket for consecutive runs, URGENT exit fires.

New safety gates (April 2026):
① Position age: URGENT suppressed until position held ≥60 min (prevents flip-flop on fresh entries).
② Probability gate: Held bucket must drop below 15% model_prob, or consensus bucket must be ≥2.5× the held bucket's probability.
③ Bid depth: Suppressed when bid depth <5 shares — cost of selling into a thin book exceeds cost of holding.
④ Adjacent grace: Consensus shifting to an adjacent bucket (±1 idx) requires 2× debounce runs — adjacent oscillation is forecast noise, not regime change.

Late-Night Alpha Window (11 PM - 6 AM ET)

11 PM - 2 AM: Lowest liquidity. Best entry prices but slowest fills. Place passive limit orders.

2 AM - 5 AM: 00z models available. If forecast shifted, edge is confirmed. Walk up to ask for faster fill.

5 AM - 7 AM: Early risers begin trading. First repricing wave. Prices may move 3-8c.

7 AM - 10 AM: Main repricing wave. Prices converge to model output. Profit-taking triggers fire.

10 AM - 12 PM: Full repricing complete. Any remaining edge is gone.

Optimal Profit-Taking Cadence (Night Owl)

+10c (or +50% of entry): Sell 50% of position. Recovers most of cost basis.

+20c (or +100% of entry): Sell 25% of position. Cost basis fully recovered.

Remaining 25% = Moon Bag: Hold to resolution. If bucket wins, pays $1.00/share — pure profit on zero cost basis.

Key Observations

  • Contracts priced 8-30c have asymmetric payoff: max loss = premium, max gain = 70-92c.
  • Overnight 00z model runs (HRRR ~1 AM, GFS ~3:30 AM ET) are not priced until 8-10 AM ET.
  • Polymarket weather market volume drops 80%+ between 11 PM and 7 AM ET.
  • Market prices at 11 PM ET have 90%+ autocorrelation with 6 AM ET prices — even when forecasts shift.
  • ECMWF 12z run (available ~6 PM ET) triggers the largest overnight repricing events.
  • Most participants use single-source forecasts (iPhone weather, Weather.com) — ensemble divergence is not priced.
  • Settlement source is Weather Underground, which can differ 1-3F from NWS/METAR due to station selection.
